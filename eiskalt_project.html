Eiskalt

Side Project

Eiskalt is a 3D Animation made by Alessio Eberl and Simon Eberl. It tells a story about a light ball escaping its natural living zone, the so called lamp and goes on a journey to explore new things. This journey however only leads to a quick end since the light seems to get smaller and lose its energy over time until it finally dies. The light was trying to escape the environment it was forced into by breaking out of its shell. Even though its only one minute long, it really takes you through a short journey in a very cold seeming environment.

The idea for this animation was inspired by the already existing song EISKALT made by Alessio Eberl. The movement and change in environment was tweaked to go along with the actual song, though there is still lots of improvement to be made. EISKALT is still a concept study and has yet to be really finished. Alessio and Simon Eberl are planning to make multiple one minute videos of this kind. “We want to create more of these, so that all one minute videos together, create one big journey switching in between different environments. We are planning on making different Biomes and other sources of light for the next projects.”
